About Us

Our Story & Mission

We believe cycling should be simple, sustainable, and fun. That’s why we make eco-friendly bike tires that never go flat, last longer, and keep riders moving.

Who We Are

Founded by engineers and cyclists, Smart Tire Company blends aerospace-grade materials science with real-world riding experience. We pioneered airless tire technology that matches — and in many cases exceeds — the comfort and control of traditional tires while eliminating the biggest frustration: flats.

Our headquarters houses a full R&D lab where prototypes are tested on urban streets, mountain trails, and high-speed loops. We partner with local riders to refine tread, casing, and lattice geometry until performance meets our strict benchmarks.

Our Mission

We aim to make cycling maintenance-free for everyone, from daily commuters to competitive athletes. Our non-pneumatic tires reduce waste by lasting years longer than standard options and being partially recyclable at end-of-life.

We want to redefine the ownership experience: no pumps, no patch kits, no roadside tube swaps — just reliable transportation and adventure whenever you roll out.

Sustainability Commitment

Every tire we produce is designed with sustainability in mind. We’ve cut manufacturing waste by 20%, use recycled materials where possible, and optimize logistics to lower our carbon footprint. Supporting eco-friendly bike tires isn’t just about ride quality — it’s about protecting the environments we ride in.
