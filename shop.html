Buying tips & sizing

Measure your current tires: diameter (e.g., 700c / 29” / 27.5”) and width (e.g., 32 mm). Check frame and fork clearances, especially on older road frames and fenders. Choose tread for the majority of your terrain: smoother for pavement, more siping for mixed paths, knobs for dirt.

Ride goals: Commuter looking for zero drama? Pick our road slick. Gravel-curious? A light all-surface pattern balances speed and bite. Hauling kids or groceries? The e-bike carcass adds stability under load and braking.

Eco-friendly bike tires: Fewer replacements = less packaging and shipping. Recyclable elements help close the loop.
