FAQs

Your Smart Tire Questions Answered

Everything from how it works to lifespan and maintenance.

How do smart airless bike tires work?

They use a solid lattice structure made from advanced polymers and SMA, eliminating the need for air pressure.

Are smart tires truly puncture-proof?

Yes, our tires cannot be punctured due to their solid design.

How long do Smart Tire Company products last?

Approximately 8,000–10,000 miles depending on use and conditions.

Are they suitable for all bikes?

We offer models for road, mountain, and electric bikes.

How is installation handled?

You can install them yourself with standard bike tools. Detailed guides are available on our website.

Do smart tires affect ride quality?

Our lattice structure provides a ride feel comparable to pneumatic tires, with added stability and durability.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal—cleaning and checking sensor readings are usually all that’s needed.
